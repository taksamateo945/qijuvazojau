Demi Lovato lip-syncs Taylor Swift and Selena Gomez with Jimmy Fallon

May 2024 · 1 minute read

Demi Lovato recently appeared on The Tonight Show starring Jimmy Fallon and participated in a segment of Lip-Sync Conversation with Demi Lovato. It’s safe to say it hit Lovatics around the world right in the feels.

The pair had a regular conversation but replied to each other using popular songs, such as “It Ain’t Me” by Lovato’s long-time friend, Selena Gomez, and Kygo, “Look What You Made Me Do” by Taylor Swift, “Hello” by Adele, and more.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1kVLpFilePw

Lovato also performed her single Sorry Not Sorry, the lead single from her sixth studio album, Tell Me You Love Me, available worldwide on September 29th.
